Cisco xStock (CSCOx) is a tracker certificate issued as Solana SPL and ERC-20 tokens. CSCOx tracks the price of Cisco Systems, Inc. (the underlying). CSCOx is designed to give eligible cryptocurrency market participants regulatory-compliant access to the stock price of Cisco Systems, Inc., whilst maintaining the benefits of blockchain technology. Cisco is a leading global technology company that focuses on networking, security, collaboration, and cloud solutions.
