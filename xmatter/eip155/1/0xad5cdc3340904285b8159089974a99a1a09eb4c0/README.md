Chevron xStock (CVXx) is a tracker certificate issued as Solana SPL and ERC-20 tokens. CVXx tracks the price of Chevron Corporation (the underlying). CVXx is designed to give eligible cryptocurrency market participants regulatory-compliant access to the stock price of Chevron Corporation, whilst maintaining the benefits of blockchain technology. Key Benefits Chevron Corporation is a major American multinational energy corporation, a leading global oil and gas company known for its vertically integrated operations, spanning exploration, production, refining, marketing, and transportation.
