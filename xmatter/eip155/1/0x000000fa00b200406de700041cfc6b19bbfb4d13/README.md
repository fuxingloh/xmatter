The Towns token ($TOWNS) is the first utility token to secure communication on the internet. By staking $TOWNS, community members help govern the ongoing flow of messaging capacity on the Towns network and earn yield in return. Fees from memberships, tips, trading, and other transactions flow back into the $TOWNS ecosystem through automatic buy and burn mechanisms, fueling the network and reinforcing community ownership.
