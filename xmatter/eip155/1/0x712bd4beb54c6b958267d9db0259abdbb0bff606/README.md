Undeads Games has been established in early 2022 as a multi-genre gaming company aimed to bring feature-rich and exciting gameplay to the crypto space. As of March 2024, Undeads Games has released Undeads Rush survival racing game for desktop and mobile devices, and Undeads Viral for Web3 players.
