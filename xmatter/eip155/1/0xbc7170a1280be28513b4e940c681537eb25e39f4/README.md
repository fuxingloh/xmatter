Comcast xStock (CMCSAx) is a tracker certificate issued as Solana SPL and ERC-20 tokens. CMCSAx tracks the price of International Comcast Corporation Class A (the underlying). CMCSAx is designed to give eligible cryptocurrency market participants regulatory-compliant access to the stock price of Comcast Corporation Class A, whilst maintaining the benefits of blockchain technology. Comcast Corporation is a global media and tech company that provides connectivity, content, and entertainment to millions of customers worldwide.
