Mastercard xStock (MAx) is a tracker certificate issued as Solana SPL and ERC-20 tokens. MAx tracks the price of Mastercard Inc. (the underlying). MAx is designed to give eligible cryptocurrency market participants regulatory-compliant access to the stock price of Mastercard Inc., whilst maintaining the benefits of blockchain technology. Mastercard Inc. is a global payments and technology company that facilitates electronic payments by connecting consumers, financial institutions, merchants, governments, and businesses worldwide.
