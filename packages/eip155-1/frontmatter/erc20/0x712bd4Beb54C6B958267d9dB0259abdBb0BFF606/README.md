# UndeadServiceToken

Undeads Games has been established in early 2022 as a multi-genre gaming company aimed to bring feature-rich and exciting gameplay to the crypto space. The company is focused on building desktop, web and mobile games with play & earn, play & own, and play & win mechanics. As of March 2024, Undeads Games has released Undeads Rush survival racing game for desktop and mobile devices, and Undeads Viral for Web3 players.
