# Broadcom tokenized stock (xStock)

Broadcom xStock (AVGOx) is a tracker certificate issued as Solana SPL and ERC-20 tokens. AVGOx tracks the price of Broadcom Inc. (the underlying). AVGOx is designed to give eligible cryptocurrency market participants regulatory-compliant access to the stock price of Broadcom Inc., whilst maintaining the benefits of blockchain technology. Key Benefits Broadcom Inc. is a global technology company that designs, develops, and supplies semiconductors and infrastructure software solutions, operating through two segments: Semiconductor Solutions and Infrastructure Software.
