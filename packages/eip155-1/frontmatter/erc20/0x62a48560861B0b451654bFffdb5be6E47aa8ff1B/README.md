# Honeywell tokenized stock (xStock)

Honeywell xStock (HONx) is a tracker certificate issued as Solana SPL and ERC-20 tokens. HONx tracks the price of Honeywell International Inc. (the underlying). HONx is designed to give eligible cryptocurrency market participants regulatory-compliant access to the stock price of Honeywell International Inc., whilst maintaining the benefits of blockchain technology. Honeywell International Inc. is an American technology and manufacturing company that operates in four core areas: Aerospace, Building Automation, Industrial Automation, and Energy and Sustainability Solutions.
