# iShares MSCI Emerging Markets ETF (Ondo Tokenized)

EEMon is the Ondo Tokenized version of the iShares MSCI Emerging Markets ETF, giving tokenholders economic exposure similar to holding EEM and reinvesting any dividends. Ondo tokenized stocks enable non-US retail and institutional users around the world to instantly mint and redeem tokenized U.S. stocks and ETFs, 24 hours a day, five days a week with full access to traditional exchange liquidity. Additional restrictions apply. Learn more at ondo.finance/global-markets.
