# Philip Morris tokenized stock (xStock)

Phillip Morris xStock (PMx) is a tracker certificate issued as Solana SPL and ERC-20 tokens. PMx tracks the price of Philip Morris International Inc. (the underlying). PMx is designed to give eligible cryptocurrency market participants regulatory-compliant access to the stock price of Philip Morris International Inc., whilst maintaining the benefits of blockchain technology. Phillip Morris is an American multinational tobacco company, with products sold in over 180 countries.
