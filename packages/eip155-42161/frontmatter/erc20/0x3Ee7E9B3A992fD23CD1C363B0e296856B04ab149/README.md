# Goldman Sachs tokenized stock (xStock)

Goldman Sachs xStock (GSx) is a tracker certificate issued as Solana SPL and ERC-20 tokens. GSx tracks the price of The Goldman Sachs Group, Inc. (the underlying). GSx is designed to give eligible cryptocurrency market participants regulatory-compliant access to the stock price of The Goldman Sachs Group, Inc., whilst maintaining the benefits of blockchain technology. Goldman Sachs is a leading global investment bank and wealth management firm.
