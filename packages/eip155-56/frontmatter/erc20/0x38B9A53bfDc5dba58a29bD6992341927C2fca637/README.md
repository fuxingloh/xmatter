# iShares MSCI EAFE ETF (Ondo Tokenized)

EFAon is the Ondo Tokenized version of the iShares MSCI EAFE ETF, giving tokenholders economic exposure similar to holding EFA and reinvesting any dividends. Ondo tokenized stocks enable non-US retail and institutional users around the world to instantly mint and redeem tokenized U.S. stocks and ETFs, 24 hours a day, five days a week with full access to traditional exchange liquidity. Additional restrictions apply. Learn more at ondo.finance/global-markets.
