# JPMorgan Chase tokenized stock (xStock)

JPMorgan Chase xStock (JPMx) is a tracker certificate issued as Solana SPL and ERC-20 tokens. JPMx tracks the price of JPMorgan Chase & Co. (the underlying). JPMx is designed to give eligible cryptocurrency market participants regulatory-compliant access to the stock price of JPMorgan Chase & Co., whilst maintaining the benefits of blockchain technology. JPMorgan Chase & Co. is a multinational financial services firm headquartered in New York City. It's a leading provider of financial services to individuals, businesses, and institutions worldwide.
