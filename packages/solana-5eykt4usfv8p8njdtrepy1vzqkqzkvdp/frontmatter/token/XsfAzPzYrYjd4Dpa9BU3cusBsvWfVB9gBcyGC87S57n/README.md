# Novo Nordisk tokenized stock (xStock)

Novo Nordisk xStock (NVOx) is a tracker certificate issued as Solana SPL and ERC-20 tokens. NVOx tracks the price of Novo Nordisk A/S, (the underlying). NVOx is designed to give eligible cryptocurrency market participants regulatory-compliant access to the stock price of Novo Nordisk A/S, whilst maintaining the benefits of blockchain technology. Novo Nordisk is a leading global healthcare company, founded in 1923 and headquartered in Denmark.
